NERAJ CHOPRA

At the Olympic Games Tokyo 2020 in 2021, Chopra entered the men's javelin competition amidst tough competition. Despite not being the outright favourite for gold, he led the qualifying round with an 86.65m throw, before dominating the final. He won gold with a throw of 87.58m and became his country's first track and field athlete to win gold at the Olympic stage.
